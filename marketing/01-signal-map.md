# Signal Map
## Turn Raw Network Data Into Instant Intelligence

---

**Tagline:** Upload. Filter. Understand. — Cellular signal analysis in seconds, not hours.

---

### The Problem
Network security teams and intelligence analysts are drowning in raw cellular measurement data. Extracting meaning from millions of signal records requires custom tooling, SQL expertise, and hours of manual analysis — before any investigation can even begin.

### What It Does
- **Instant visualization** — Upload a CSV and see thousands of signal measurements rendered on an interactive map in seconds, as a heatmap or individual data points
- **Smart filtering** — Slice data by operator, PLMN (country code), ISO country, or time range with a single click — no code required
- **One-click forensic reports** — Generate a classified, bilingual (English/Hebrew) intelligence briefing from any filtered view, ready to share with command
- **Anomaly spotting** — Visual density patterns immediately surface unusual concentrations, coverage gaps, and operator footprints that raw data hides
- **Time-range analysis** — Built-in histogram lets analysts zoom into specific windows of activity

### Who It's For
- Network security analysts and SIGINT professionals
- Telecom operators investigating coverage or interference
- Law enforcement and regulatory bodies reviewing field measurements
- Intelligence teams preparing briefings from crowdsourced data

### Why It's Different
Most signal analysis tools require a data engineer to set up pipelines, write queries, and build dashboards before an analyst sees anything. Signal Map eliminates that bottleneck — any analyst with a CSV file is operational in under a minute. The integrated forensic report generator means findings go from map to briefing without leaving the platform.

### Scale & Performance
- Handles **hundreds of thousands of data points** client-side with no backend
- Tested against data from **530M+ measurements** across 10+ countries
- Supports all major cellular generations: **5G NR, LTE, WCDMA, GSM**
