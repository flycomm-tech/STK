# Spectra
## Real-Time RF Threat Detection. Anywhere. Always On.

---

**Tagline:** The platform that sees what others don't — distributed RF intelligence for organizations that can't afford to be blind.

---

### The Problem
IMSI-catchers, GPS jammers, rogue base stations, and electronic warfare equipment operate silently. By the time a threat is detected through traditional means, the damage is done — identities exposed, communications intercepted, operations compromised. Organizations protecting sensitive sites, personnel, and infrastructure need continuous, automated RF awareness — not periodic audits.

### What It Does
- **Continuous threat detection** — Spectra's distributed sensor network monitors the RF environment 24/7, automatically detecting IMSI-catchers, GPS spoofing, forced 2G downgrades, wide-band jamming, and rogue base stations in real time
- **Distributed RSU hardware** — Purpose-built RF monitoring nodes (Remote Sensing Units) deploy across your coverage area, organized into clusters. Each unit monitors independently and reports to the central platform
- **Intelligent alert engine** — Every alert includes severity (Critical/High/Medium), confidence score, affected frequency band, measured deviation, and a plain-language description — giving operators context, not just notifications
- **Tactical map** — Real-time visualization of all RSU positions, alert origins, and threat locations on an interactive map with timeline playback for post-incident analysis
- **Analytics & baselines** — Spectral waterfall history, cluster health scoring, and baseline-vs-current comparison enable trend analysis and early warning before active attacks
- **Enterprise management** — Centralized RSU inventory, firmware lifecycle management, cluster organization, and multi-tenant organization isolation — all through a single platform

### Who It's For
- Government and defense agencies protecting facilities and personnel from electronic surveillance
- Telecom operators monitoring for unauthorized transmitters and rogue infrastructure
- Critical infrastructure operators (airports, power grids, data centers) requiring RF perimeter security
- Law enforcement and counter-intelligence units conducting surveillance detection
- Corporate security teams protecting executives, boardrooms, and sensitive operations from IMSI-catchers

### Why It's Different
Spectra is not a generic spectrum analyzer or a passive log-correlation tool. It is an active, distributed, intelligent detection system purpose-built for modern cellular threats. The combination of hardware (RSU nodes) and software (AI-assisted detection engine) means Spectra detects subtle attacks — TAC mismatches, timing anomalies, constellation divergence — that signal-strength-only tools miss entirely. Seven pre-configured attack patterns cover the full threat landscape out of the box, and custom rules adapt to unique operational environments.

### Scale & Performance
- Designed for **enterprise and national-scale deployments** with multi-tenant architecture
- Each RSU captures **GPS, signal, device, and network telemetry simultaneously**
- Alert-to-operator latency measured in **seconds** via real-time streaming
- Backed by a data infrastructure processing **530M+ historical measurements** for baseline modeling
- Supports **5G NR, LTE, WCDMA, and GSM** threat detection across all frequency bands
