# Roaming QoS Report
## See the World Through Your Subscribers' Signal

---

**Tagline:** International roaming intelligence — understand network quality, detect anomalies, and protect subscribers abroad.

---

### The Problem
When subscribers roam internationally, operators lose visibility. You don't know if they're experiencing degraded service, being forced onto legacy networks, or exposed to surveillance infrastructure in foreign countries. Roaming complaints are reactive. Security incidents go undetected. Regulators are asking questions you can't answer.

### What It Does
- **Roaming-specific analysis** — Filters exclusively to international roaming measurements, isolating foreign network experience from domestic data
- **Per-country QoS benchmarking** — Compare signal quality (RSRP, RSRQ), download speed, upload speed, and latency across every visited country and operator
- **Technology downgrade detection** — Identify when subscribers are being forced from 5G/LTE onto legacy WCDMA or GSM networks — a key indicator of surveillance infrastructure
- **Home carrier tracking** — Understand which of your subscriber groups roam where, and how each group's experience compares across destinations
- **Forensic report generation** — One-click export of classified intelligence briefing covering all roaming findings, ready for regulatory submission or executive review
- **IMSI movement analysis** — Detect unusual subscriber movement patterns indicative of espionage activity or targeted tracking

### Who It's For
- International telecom operators managing roaming agreements and SLA compliance
- Roaming and wholesale teams benchmarking partner network quality
- SIGINT analysts tracking foreign subscriber presence and movement
- Regulatory compliance teams documenting subscriber protection in foreign markets
- Corporate security teams protecting executive and sensitive personnel abroad

### Why It's Different
Roaming QoS Report is built specifically for the international dimension — it doesn't just filter to roaming records, it understands what matters in a roaming context: technology tier, partner network reliability, geographic exposure, and subscriber trajectory. The combination of QoS benchmarking, technology analysis, and forensic report generation in a single tool is unique. It turns reactive roaming complaints into proactive intelligence.

### Scale & Performance
- Queries live against **530M+ measurement rows** with roaming flag isolation
- Covers roaming measurements across **10+ visited countries**
- Supports **all cellular generations** — 5G NR, LTE, WCDMA, GSM — with technology-aware benchmarking
- Generates **classified forensic reports** in both English and Hebrew
